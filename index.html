Using Clay For Construction


The majority of the earth's crust consists of clay, created by the weathering of feldspar-rich rocks.

Clay has been used for centuries as a construction material. Even today, buildings are still around that were built with clay hundreds of years ago.

The building material clay from demolition can easily be reused. Clay is a building material with a recycling rate of 100 percent


What makes Clay the construction material of the future?

  • Clay is locally available everywhere and therefore saves energy and resources.
  • It is reusable.
  • It has pleasant surface temparatures.
  • It does not release polutants.
  • Regulates air humidity.
  • Provides sound insulation.
